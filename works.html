02 : About Boston

Boston has a lot of Beautiful spots. Please let me introduce part of them.

001/006

Museum of Fine Arts, Boston

This museum is one of the biggest museums in the world. It has over half a million works. A lot of Japanese traditional arts are exhibited and it also have Japanese traditional garden “Tenshinen.”

Discover

002/006

Boston Public Garden

This garden is the oldest public botanical garden in the USA. It has a flower garden, rose garden and big pond and you can ride swan boats there. In summer, you can watch real swan in the pond.

Discover

003/006

Boston Public Library

This is the oldest public library in the USA. There are about 30 pictures in this library and the murals and the painting on the ceiling are really beautiful so it looks like museum. Here is admission free!

Discover

004/006

Prudential Center Skywalk

There is a skywalk observatory on the 50th floor and you can enjoy the beautiful view of Boston. On the 52th floor, you can enjoy having delicious meals in a restaurant with beautiful view.

Discover

005/006

Freedom Trail

There are 16 historical sites along the trail. You can visit the sites about American independence, so it is recommended to go there first. However, your have to walk about 6km and it takes half a day. Please prepare well.

Discover

006/006

Boston Common

This is the oldest park in America. It is the starting point of Freedom Trail. You can buy some pamphlets, maps of Boston and souvenirs. In winter, you can enjoy ice skating at Frog pond.
